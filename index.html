Salesforce Administrator Course Overview

The Salesforce Administrator course is designed to provide practical, job-oriented expertise in managing and configuring Salesforce CRM applications to meet real-world business requirements.

Salesforce & CRM Fundamentals

Build a strong foundation in CRM principles and Salesforce architecture, including Sales Cloud, Service Cloud, and the Lightning Experience used by modern enterprises.

User Management and Security Model

Learn how to manage users, assign licenses, and control data access using profiles, roles, permission sets, organization-wide defaults, and sharing rules.

Data Modeling: Objects, Fields, and Relationships

Understand how Salesforce data is structured using standard and custom objects, fields, and relationships such as lookup and master-detail to design scalable data models.

Data Management and Data Quality

Work with Data Import Wizard and Data Loader while applying validation rules and duplicate management to maintain high data quality standards.

Business Process Automation with Flow Builder

Automate approvals, notifications, record updates, and task assignments using Flow Builder without writing code.

Reports, Dashboards, and Business Insights

Create meaningful reports and dashboards to enable data-driven decision-making for management and stakeholders.

Application Customization with Lightning App Builder

Customize record pages, home pages, app pages, page layouts, record types, and tabs to enhance usability and productivity.

Email Configuration and Activity Management

Configure email templates, alerts, tasks, and events to improve communication and activity tracking within Salesforce.

AppExchange and Basic Integrations

Learn to install and manage AppExchange applications and understand the basics of Salesforce integrations with external systems.

Live Project: Real-Time CRM Implementation

Apply knowledge in a simulated real-world CRM use case covering sales and service processes.

Certification and Interview Preparation

Prepare for the Salesforce Administrator Certification exam with mock tests, scenario-based questions, and interview guidance.

Career Opportunities

Qualify for roles such as Salesforce Administrator, CRM Administrator, Support Executive, Junior Consultant, and Operations Executive.

This course focuses on practical learning, real-time application, and industry-relevant skills to ensure readiness for a Salesforce Administrator role.
