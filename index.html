Salesforce Administrator Course Overview

The Salesforce Administrator course is designed to provide practical, job-oriented expertise in managing and configuring Salesforce CRM applications to meet real-world business needs. As the world‚Äôs leading cloud-based Customer Relationship Management platform, Salesforce is widely used by organizations to streamline sales, service, marketing, and customer data operations. This course introduces learners to the Salesforce ecosystem, CRM fundamentals, and the Lightning Experience interface used by modern enterprises.


Throughout the course, participants gain hands-on experience in key administrative areas such as user management, security controls, data modeling, data management, business process automation with Flow Builder, and application customization using Lightning App Builder. Learners also develop the ability to create reports and dashboards that provide meaningful business insights, ensuring they can support data-driven decision-making within an organization.


By the end of the program, learners will be prepared to handle real-time Salesforce administration scenarios and confidently pursue the Salesforce Administrator Certification. The course emphasizes practical implementation, live project exposure, and interview preparation, enabling participants to step into roles such as Salesforce Administrator, CRM Admin, or Salesforce Support Executive with confidence and industry-ready skills.
